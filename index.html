Face Recognition and Gender Classification

  • Michael was tasked to build a system for face recognition and gender classification from scratch in his Introduction to Machine Learning Course.

  • The system used a simple linear regression classification algorithm which could distinguish between the faces of Steve Carell and Bill Hader and between the faces of Male and Females.

  • One-Hot encoding was also implemented to enable multiclassification of different actors.
